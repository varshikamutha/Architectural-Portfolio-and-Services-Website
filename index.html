Bit About Me

Hello! My name is Harshika Mutha, and I have completed my Bachelor of Architecture from Sinhgad College of Architecture, Pune. Over the past five years, my architectural journey has shaped a deeper perspective on life and brought me closer to nature and its influence on design. I am now seeking a job opportunity to further broaden this perspective by applying my knowledge in practical, real-world contexts. I aim to refine my skills and contribute meaningfully to the field of architecture. As part of my academic experience, I also completed a six-month internship at Design Studio, Lonavala, under the guidance of Ar. Shabbir Unwala, where I gained valuable insights into professional practice and design execution.
